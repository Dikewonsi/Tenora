Tenora MVP Scope

Core workflow:

Property -> Units -> Tenants -> Tenancy/Lease Assignment -> Service Charge Budget
-> Calculate Schedule -> Review -> Issue Demands -> Record Payments

Tenancy dates:

- start_date is the occupancy start.
- end_date is both the tenancy end date and next rent due date.
- Rent expiry is shown automatically in Expiring Soon, 30 Days, 60 Days, and
  90 Days buckets.
- Admins do not manually schedule rent reminders.

Service charges:

- Flat rate: total budget / number of units.
- Pro rata: total budget * unit floor area / total property unit area.
- Approved budgets issue one demand per occupied unit allocation.
- Demand preview and payments use the issued allocation amount.
